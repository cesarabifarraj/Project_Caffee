ABOUT US

At Caffée, "we believe that a great cup of coffee is more than just a morning routine—it’s an art form. From the high-altitude farms of Ethiopia to your favorite mug, every bean we roast is handled with precision and care. We partner directly with sustainable growers to ensure every sip is ethically sourced and bursting with unique, complex flavors. Whether you're a fan of a bright pour-over or a silky, hand-poured latte, we’re here to elevate your coffee experience, one bean at a time."
